What is this?

This is a website that has every single map on Splatoon 2 Shifty Station!
Feel free to use the images as your reference.

What is Shifty Station?

Shifty Station is a series of different maps that can be only played during Splatfests in Splatoon 2, and the maps of Shifty Station will be randomised everytime you play it!
